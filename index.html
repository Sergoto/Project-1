How to Play IMDB Trivia Game


Welcome movie lovers! This is the IMDB Rating Trivia game.

In this game you'll get a random movie to rate as your question along with 4 different movie ratings to choose from

(Ratings from 1.0 to 10.0)


Pick the rating you think is the IMDB rating and see if you're right, if you were close, or if you were way off.
Time to put your movie taste to the test against the rest of the internet!
